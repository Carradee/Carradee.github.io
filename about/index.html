Publication is about communication. It doesn’t matter if it’s a book, a website, or an app—you’re communicating something, whether it’s instructions how to do something or that your game is worth playing.

Communication is what you say and how you say it. Maybe you say it directly via an essay or book, or maybe you show it with a story or a game.

That’s the “big picture” of how it works: General style and medium.

There’s also the “little picture”, which gets down to the specifics of what you include, what you omit, and how you present it.
